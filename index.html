Research

Research Summary: Feasibility of AI in Orthodontic Treatment Planning

A study was conducted to evaluate the feasibility, accuracy, and reliability of using commercially available large language models (LLMs), such as ChatGPT and Bard (now Gemini), in orthodontic treatment planning. The AI-generated treatment plans were assessed against gold-standard treatment plans developed by board-certified orthodontists. Both sets of plans utilized comprehensive patient data, including medical history and records. To ensure a robust evaluation, a custom grading scale was developed to systematically compare the quality, precision, and comprehensiveness of the treatment plans. The results highlighted the potential of LLMs to assist in orthodontic decision-making, while also revealing areas where AI fell short of the expertise demonstrated by human professionals. This research contributes to the understanding of AI's role in enhancing efficiency and accuracy in dental care.
